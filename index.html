Take the work out of documenting your work.


The Problem


Knowledge silos cause mistakes, delays, and redundant communication.


Documentation helps prevent knowledge siloing, but is time-consuming and hard to do.

The Solution


Noted creates documentation passively, without making you change your habits, so that you can work on what's important.


Noted collects and organizes communications that are hard to record and share, and organizes them in your team's wiki.

The Solution


The initial product collects and records information from in-person meetings and conversations.

Technology


Noted uses parallelized, human-corrected machine transciption to produce transcripts as accurately as a human in a fraction of the time and for a fraction of the cost.

Technology


Human transcription costs $1/minute, and 1 minute of audio may take more than 4 minutes to transcribe.


Noted's human-corrected transcription could cost less than $0.25/minute, while retaining good accuracy.


30 minutes of audio could be transcribed in less than 1 minute if enough workers are available.
